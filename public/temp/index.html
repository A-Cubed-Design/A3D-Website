The Three A's of A Cubed

The three A's of A Cubed Design, Accessible, Affordable, and Accelerated are the principles on which we operate and grow from.

Accessible

We value accessibility first and foremost
 We aim to provide a product, which will be easily usable and well documented

Affordable

Our braille display is slated to become the most easily obtainable product on the market

Accelerated

We aim to accelerate the way blind individuals interface with their environment by adding plug and play features to our device
